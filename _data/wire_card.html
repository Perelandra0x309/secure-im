Wire is a messenger which has it's primary focus on enterprise use but it is available for use by individuals for free at this time. The app is based in Switzerland but does get some funding from U.S. venture capitalists so this is something to just keep an eye on. It is a reasonable alternative to Threema if you are looking for a few specific features, such as using one account on multiple devices, using it on a desktop computer or if you need video chat with more than one other person.


Pros:
  • Communicate with text, audio (25 max) and video (12 max) group chat
  • Optional automatic deletion of message up to 4 weeks after arrival
  • Based in Switzerland (great privacy laws)
  • Perfect forward secrecy
  • Use on multiple devices including phones and desktop computers
Cons:
  • Need a phone number or email to signup
  • Received venture capital funding from the U.S.
Other features:
  • Send photos, videos, audio clips, files and location
  • Search for words in a chat
  • Like a comment
  • Recall a message to delete it from all participants' devices
  • Use up to 8 devices with one account
  • Contact verification by comparing session fingerprints
