Features self destruct messages and deletion of messages from all devices.
Sending a location shows up wrong for the recipient, when we tested it was not even in the right country.
The missed call log creates duplicate entries each time you go into it.
No notifications of new messages on Android without Google Play services.
Most egregious of all is when registering your email becomes your ID.
My verdict:
While many of the bugs found can be fixed, there is no getting around the fact that your email address is exposed as your user ID. That makes this app a no go for me.
